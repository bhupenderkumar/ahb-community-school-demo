Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers use a play-based approach to stimulate creativity and curiosity.

🏫

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-5, emphasizing core subjects like reading, writing, mathematics, and science. We also offer a range of electives, including art, music, and physical education.

📚

Middle/Upper School

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students in grades 6-12, offering a rigorous college-preparatory curriculum with a range of Advanced Placement courses. Our experienced teachers guide students in developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills.
